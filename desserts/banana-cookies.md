# Banana cookies

## Ingredients

- 125 g butter
- 100 g flour
- 1 package walnuts or other nuts
- 1 Tsp. baking soda (Natron)
- 350 g Bananas (weighed including peel)
- 1 pinch of salt


## Instructions

- Mash Bananas
- Mix everything
- Use a tablespoon to put dough onto a baking sheet and flatten the dough portions
- bake the, at 160 °C with circulating air in the oven
